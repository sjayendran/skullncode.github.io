The Creators

Graphicurry has been the pioneer with pop art in India amongst the youth. After reaching out to its audience with one of its kind artworks of movie, music and television; Graphicurry has stepped into yet another league of mobile game apps.

The founder and artist Prasad Bhat who has always been fond of food, decided to join his best pal and chief coder Sandeep Jayendran to bring out Run Cal !

The happy, catchy music that will stay in your head is by music composer Sandesh Nagaraj who has studied media composition at California State University, Northridge and Musician’s Institute, LA.
