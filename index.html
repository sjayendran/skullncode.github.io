About the game

Run while you have fun! Trying to be fit in real? Start with Cal. He’s on the run and has pledged to eat only healthy food!

Run with Cal and make sure you just don’t touch the junk food at all. After all, how will you or Cal get fit if you can’t avoid the junk food?!

We at Graphicurry have vouched to make this world a better place with promoting healthier choices of food. More than anything, we want to get everyone moving and burning calories.

Just like Cal! It’s easy, it’s fun. Just RUN!

About the creators

Graphicurry has been the pioneer with pop art in India amongst the youth.

After reaching out to its audience with one of its kind artworks and merchandise of movie, music and television; Graphicurry has stepped into yet another league of mobile game apps. The founder and artist Prasad Bhat who has always been fond of food, decided to join his best pal and chief coder Sandeep Jayendran to bring out Run Cal!

The happy, catchy music that will stay in your head is by music composer Sandesh Nagaraj who has studied media composition at California State University, Northridge and Musician’s Institute, LA.

The content writing was added by the humble fourth wheel to the cart, Deepti Kharbanda.

Last but not the least, the game could not have been released without our amazing testers who volunteered to get active and get ‘running’ with Cal.
